What are the Bristol Archives?

The Bristol Archives store thousands of records dating as far back as 10 centuries. Our job is to preserve and document these items and therefore we have been digitising them to enable easier access to the public. This website will enable you to search for the items we have digitised and view them freely. We continue to digitise records and continue to grow our collection.

How do I search the Bristol Archives?

There are Two main ways to search the Bristol Archive entries, either enter a what you want to view in the search bar on the home page, or use the advanced search which will narrow down the results to make it easier to find what you are looking for. If Your search does not return the result you were looking for it is possible it has not been digitised yet.
